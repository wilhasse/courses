# Lessons

## Chapter 1 Survival Essentials

### Vertical Movement

j - down  
k - up  
x - delete caracter

### Horizontal Movement

h - left  
l - right

### Introduction to Modes

i - insert mode  
ESC - normal mode

### Text Objects in Vim

w - next word  
shift + w - ignore punctuation  
b - previous word  
shift + b - ignore punctuation

### Word Based Movement

e - end of the word  
shift + e - treat punctuation as the same word
